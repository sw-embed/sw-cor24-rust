In your Fibonacci example, you ARE waiting for TX not busy, you do it this way:

.wait:
        lb      r2,1(r1)        ; read status byte
        lcu     r0,128
        and     r2,r0           ; isolate bit 7
        ceq     r2,z
        brf     .wait           ; spin while TX busy

but I would do it this way:

.wait:
        lb      r2,1(r1)        ; read status byte  (8 bit sign-extended)
        cls     r2,z
        brt     .wait           ; spin while TX busy

I put the TX busy status bit on bit 7 just for this purpose.
